How To Management Time

5 Tips to help you mange your time bettre

  1. Prioritize

    2. Know your priorities and plan to implement them and what suits the nature of your day. It is best to combine similar activities between you and yourself so that they do not take up time.

  2. Creat a daily Schedule
  3. Stay organized

    4. Time management cannot be achieved without organization. Organization is an important way to manage time successfully, so try to make your goal specific in terms of big and small tasks. Give everything its due.

  4. Do your most important tasks in the morning
  5. Get rid of stress

    6. Stress and anxiety make your mind think negatively, so get rid of anxiety and any negative influences that cause your day to stop, by doing some exercise during your day.
